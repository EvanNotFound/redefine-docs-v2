# Basic Information `info`

## Title

**Configuration Item Name: `info.title`**

The site title, displayed in the web page title bar.

![image-20230321211443153](https://evan.beee.top/img/2023/03/21/5581ab5daa266c984726b53b2b65c18d.png)

## Subtitle

**Configuration Item Name: `info.subtitle`**

Site subtitle, displayed after the site title on the homepage.

![preview](https://assets.ohevan.com/img/f2c24a4f9d23ac4d7d5d896f207fdbc5.jpg)

## Author

**Configuration Item Name: `info.author`**

The site author, displayed as the article author and at the bottom of the site.

![image-20230321211749089](https://evan.beee.top/img/2023/03/21/23756538a05d965d3de3d9fdf1a1f547.png)
![image-20230321212008343](https://evan.beee.top/img/2023/03/21/179d335fbf45c88b32b62c30b2a8830c.png)

## Site URL

**Configuration Item Name: `info.url`**

Used to concatenate links for articles, copyright information, etc. Please fill in correctly, without adding `/` at the end of the link.
