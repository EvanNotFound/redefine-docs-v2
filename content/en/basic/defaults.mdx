# Default Images `defaults`

## Site Icon

**Configuration Item: `defaults.favicon`**

Please specify the path to your site icon, such as `/images/favicon.ico` or an external link `https://example.com/favicon.ico`.

![Screenshot 2023-03-21 at 9.34.23 PM](https://evan.beee.top/img/2023/03/21/2230cd8f61c9e07109113aab9539d8cf.png)

## Site Logo

**Configuration Item: `defaults.logo`**

Displayed in the top left corner of the navigation bar. Can be left empty.

![image-20230321213723678](https://evan.beee.top/img/2023/03/21/cff6f1d8714a6e801806d564886c9c9a.png)

## Avatar

**Configuration Item: `defaults.avatar`**

Avatar displayed in the author information on the article page.
